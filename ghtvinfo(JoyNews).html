Joy News

Joy News is a 24-hour news channel in Ghana started in 2010. It is the first of its kind in the country and its operated by the Multimedia Group. With over a decade of being in the media space, the channel is widely known for;

  • Its riveting news coverage in Ghana with up-to-date information of the big stories.
  • Comprehensive election coverage powered by its sister platforms like Joy 99.7 FM.
  • Compelling current affairs programming that brings the powerful to book.

Vist www.myjoyonline.com for the day's major stories from JoyNews as well as live stream of the station. Also you can get interactive on their social media platforms.
