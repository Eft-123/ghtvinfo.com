Onua TV

Onua TV, a subsidiary of Media General, is a fast-growing local language television station in Ghana initiated in 2019. It's well known in the country for its;

  • Intriguing entertainment content.
  • Exiting lifestyle programming.
  • Compelling news coverage powered by its sister station, TV3 Ghana.
  • Riveting sports programs.

Vist www.onuaonline.com for news from Onua TV. Also you can get interactive on their media platforms.
