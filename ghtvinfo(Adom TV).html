Adom TV

Adom TV is a popular local-language speaking television station in Ghana - the first of its kind since 2009. It is well-recognized in the country for its;

  • Intriguing news content.
  • Riveting lifestyle programs.
  • Diverse entertainment programming.
  • Foreign novella content.

Vist www.adomonline.com for news from Adom TV as well as live stream of the station. Also you can get interactive on their media platforms.
